Work Income Tracker - Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how the Work Income Tracker extension handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information remains secure.

Data Collection and Storage

The Work Income Tracker extension collects and stores the following data:

  • Your monthly salary amount (as entered by you)
  • Your work hours (start time and end time as configured by you)
  • Work session information (when you start and end work)
  • Language preference settings

Important: All data is stored locally on your device using the Chrome storage API. No data is ever transmitted to external servers, collected by us, or shared with third parties.

Use of Data

The data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To calculate your real-time income during work hours
  • To provide progress tracking of your workday
  • To maintain your preferred language settings
  • To ensure work session continuity between browser sessions

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere, the security risk is minimal. Your sensitive salary information never leaves your browser.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can view, modify, or delete any information stored by the extension through the extension settings. If you uninstall the extension, all stored data will be completely removed from your device.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be included with extension updates.
